## Multi-Passage Rotary Union System

### Video Demonstration
- **Kadant Rotary Union Overview**: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IOLcnCO3iOM
  - Demonstrates how multi-passage rotary unions enable fluid and electrical transfer between stationary and rotating components
  - Critical technology for transferring water, sewage, power, and data to rotating city rings
